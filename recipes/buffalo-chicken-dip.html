Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description:

Buffalo chicken dip is a creamy and tangy appetizer that captures the flavors and heat of Buffalo chicken wings. Made with just five ingredients, it’s the perfect party appetizer, delivering all the tantalizing flavors of buffalo chicken without the mess of sticky fingers! You can either cook the chicken ahead of time or use rotisserie chicken to make it extra easy. It’s not a competition, but you’ve definitely won the appetizer game with this one!

Ingredients:

  • 4 frozen ~4 ounce chicken breasts
  • 2 package (16 ounces) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot Original Cayenne Pepper Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:

  1. Place frozen chicken breasts and 1 1/2 cups of water into the Instant Pot. Close the valve and set to manual mode for 11 minutes.
  2. Once the timer finishes, allow the pressure to naturally release for 8 minutes before carefully releasing the remaining pressure.
  3. Shred the chicken using a handheld mixer directly in the Instant Pot for quick and easy shredding.
  4. Transfer all ingredients to the crockpot, set on low, and stir every 20 minutes until ready to serve.
